## 👋 About Me

I am a **System, Infrastructure and DevOps-focused IT Specialist** working on enterprise systems, cloud services, automation, monitoring, backup and cybersecurity operations.

My focus is to design, operate and improve systems that are **secure, observable, automated and recoverable**. I work with on-premise infrastructure, Microsoft Azure, GitHub-based workflows, CI/CD practices, monitoring platforms, backup systems and security technologies.

I also build scripts, internal tools and automation workflows for real business needs, including ERP-connected internal systems, database-driven applications, operational dashboards and production-side integrations.

My experience connects both sides of IT operations: cloud and DevOps practices on one side, and hands-on enterprise infrastructure, SAP/SQL systems, RFID/barcode workflows, backup platforms and edge devices on the other.
